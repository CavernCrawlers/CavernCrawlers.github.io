## Statistics
- **Starting HP**: 5
- **Hit Dice**: 1d5 per level
- **Initiative Bonus**: +1
- **Armor Training**: Light armor
- **Weapon Training**: Light and Medium weapons

## Equipment
- a Rogue's Pack, a Mage's Pack or an Explorer's Pack
- 2 Sigils
- 4d6sp
