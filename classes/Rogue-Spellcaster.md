# Rogue-Spellcaster

---
## Statistics
- **Starting HP**: 5
- **Hit Dice (Levels 2 to 10)**: 1d5 per level
- **HP for Levels 11+:** +1/level
- **Armor Training**: Light armor
- **Weapon Training**: Light and Medium weapons

---
## Equipment
- an [Adventurer's Pack](EncumbranceAndEquipment.md#equipment%20packs)
- a [Trickster's Pack](EncumbranceAndEquipment.md#equipment%20packs)
- 2d6sp
