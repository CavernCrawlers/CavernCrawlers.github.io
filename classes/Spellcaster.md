## Statistics
- **Starting HP**: 4 + CON Modifier
- **Hit Dice**: 1d4 + CON Modifier per level from 2 to 10
- **HP after level 10:** +1 per level (no CON modifier)
- **Initiative Bonus**: +0
- **Armor Training**: None
- **Weapon Training**: Light weapons & Staffs

## Equipment
- a Mage's Pack
- 4 Sigils
- 4d6sp
