## Statistics
- **Starting HP**: 6 + CON Modifier
- **Hit Dice**: 1d6 + CON Modifier per level
- **HP after level 10:** +2 per level.
- **Initiative Bonus**: +0
- **Armor Training**: Light and Medium Armor, Buckler and Shield
- **Weapon Training**: Light and Medium weapons

## Equipment
- an Explorer's Pack
- a Spellbook
- 2 Sigils
- 4d6sp
