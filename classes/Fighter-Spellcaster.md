## Statistics
- **Starting HP**: 6 + CON Modifier
- **Hit Dice (Levels 2 to 10)**: 1d6 + CON Modifier per level
- HP for Levels 11+: +1/level
- **Armor Training**: Light and Medium Armor, Buckler and Shield
- **Weapon Training**: Light and Medium weapons

- an [Adventurer's Pack](EncumbranceAndEquipment.md#equipment%20packs)

## Sample Fighter-Spellcaster Builds
- **Spellblade**
	- 1st Level: Tradition - Wizardry, Skirmisher
	- 2nd Level: [Infuse Weapon](Traits.md#infuse%20weapon)
	- 5th Level: Weapon Specialization
- **Forest Warden**
	- 1st Level: Tradition - Animism, Precise Shot
	- 2nd Level: [Child of the {Terrain}](Traits.md#child%20of%20the%20{terrain})
	- 5th Level: Archer
- **Warlock**
	- 1st Level: Tradition - Witchcraft, Deadly Blow
	- 2nd Level: [Ghost-Touched](Traits.md#ghost-touched)
	- 5th Level: Cleave
- **Warrior-Priest**
	- 1st Level: Tradition - Thaumaturgy, Indomitable
	- 2nd Level: [Healing Touch](Traits.md#healing%20touch)
	- 5th Level: Cleave
