## Statistics
- **Starting HP**: 8 + CON Modifier
- **Hit Dice**: 1d6 + 2 + CON Modifier per level from 2 to 10.
- **HP after level 10**: +2 per level.
- **Initiative Bonus**: +1
- **Armor Training**: All
- **Weapon Training**: All

## Equipment
- a Warrior's Pack or an Explorer's Pack
- 4d6sp
