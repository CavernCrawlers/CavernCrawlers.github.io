# The Barbarian Namer
## ([Spellcaster](classes/Spellcaster.md))
In your homeland, you were a child apprentice of the tribeâ€™s Namer, from whom you learned many secrets and the power of names. After being separated from your people and your ancestral lands, you found a new home in the village. You still keep the stories and secrets of your people, though now  you share them with new friends.  You gain access to the [Namer's (Wizard's) Apprentice](optional/Background.md#wizard's%20Apprentice) background.

## Childhood
You are durable and wise. Your Constitution and Wisdom begin at 10, and all of your other ability scores begin at 8.

## Apprenticeship
You learned the secrets of your people. You become a level 1 [Spellcaster](classes/Spellcaster.md). You gain the class abilities *Magical Focus*, *Spellcasting* and *Sense Magic*.  You also learn the [Beast Speech](magic/TierZeroSpells.md#beast%20speech) cantrip and the Beast [sigil](magic/Sigils.md).  The tables below will tell you your other abilities.

## Starting Equipment
The Barbarian Namer begins with the following equipment: your background trappings, a dagger, animal-skin clothing, 1 week's provisions, a spellbook (with 1 additional random tier 2 spell, which you cannot yet cast), a backpack, a bedroll, a mess kit, a tinderbox, 10 torches, 50ft rope, one light weapon and 2d6sp.  You also have any items gained from your Lifepath.
